Smith Machine Squat Overview

The Smith Machine squat primarily targets the following muscles:
1-Quadriceps (front of the thighs)
2-Glutes (buttocks)
3-Hamstrings (back of the thighs)
4-Adductors (inner thighs)
5-Core muscles (to stabilize the body)
The Smith Machine provides more stability, so it can reduce the need for balance, allowing for more focus on these muscles.

Dumbbell Cossack Squat Overview

Dumbbell Cossack Squat primarily works the following muscles:
1-Quadriceps (front of the thighs)
2-Glutes (buttocks)
3-Hamstrings (back of the thighs)
4-Adductors (inner thighs)
5-Hip Flexors
6-Core muscles (for stability and balance)
The exercise emphasizes the inner thighs (adductors) due to the wide stance and deep squat movement, while also engaging the quads and glutes as you lower your body.

High Knee Lunge on Bosu Ball Overview

The High Knee Lunge on Bosu Ball primarily works the following muscles:
1-Quadriceps (front of the thighs)
2-Glutes (buttocks)
3-Hamstrings (back of the thighs)
4-Hip Flexors (lifting the knee)
5-Core Muscles (for stability and balance)
The Bosu ball adds instability, requiring more activation from the core and stabilizing muscles, while the high knee movement targets the hip flexors and helps improve balance.

Dumbbell Walking Lunge Overview

The Dumbbell Walking Lunge primarily works the following muscles:
1-Quadriceps (front of the thighs)
2-Glutes (buttocks)
3-Hamstrings (back of the thighs)
4-Hip Flexors (for stepping forward)
5-Core Muscles (for stability and balance)
The exercise targets the quads and glutes as you step forward, while the hamstrings and hip flexors assist in the movement. The core is engaged to maintain balance as you walk.
